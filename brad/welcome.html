Our purpose is to equip believers with a theological foundation so they can grow in the knowledge of Christ to effectively reach their peers or communities with the gospel.

Serving in college ministry for fifteen years I agree with other prominent evangelical pastors and leaders that Christians are starving for biblical truth. Never before in the history of the world have more resources been available yet there are so many misconceptions about God and the gospel.

What is the result?
A watered down gospel,
an easy-believism theology, and
a misunderstanding of the doctrine of God.
Living in a college town, my ministry focus includes a focus on international college students and young adults. College students come to East Carolina from all over the world. My prayer is that these international students will believe the gospel, become equipped in biblical theology, communicate the foundational doctrines of Christianity effectively, and become trained to use their gifts to effectively reach others in their home countries.

In-Depth Studies is also a resource to Christians who want to grow in their theological development which includes discipleship, speaking opportunities, skype meetings, and writing bible study material. In addition, our ministry has partnerships internationally in Canada, Czech Republic, Austria, Romania, Great Britain, Mexico, and Belarus. We partner with local churches in these countries to equip pastors, campus ministries, and lay leaders. As a result, these churches are effectively reaching their communities, sending missionaries, and planting churches globally.

My prayer for our ministry is the same as Paul’s in Ephesians 3:17-19: that you being rooted and grounded in love, may have strength to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth, and to know the love of Christ that surpasses knowledge, that you may be filled with all the fullness of God.
